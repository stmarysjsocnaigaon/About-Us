Our History

Beginnings

The history of St. Mary’s Jacobite Syrian Church, Naigaon is linked to St. Thomas Syrian Church Congregation, Byculla (est. 1975). Families settled across Mira Road, Bhayandar, Naigaon, Vasai, Nallasopara, and Virar. For Holy Qurbana and spiritual needs, they initially depended on Byculla and Malad.

Foundation of the Church

First Holy Qurbana – 29th Nov 1987 by Rev. Fr. Varghese Valayil. Vasai became independent in 1998 with Rev. Fr. Kuriakose Kadavumbhagam as vicar. Land (65 cents) donated by Mr. Antony John Pereira via Chev. K. V. John’s effort to build the Church cum Community Center.

Present & Parish Activities

Parish has over 100 families. Services every Sunday at St. Mary’s, Naigaon, and at St. George Service Center, Nallasopara (2nd & 4th Sunday). Wednesday prayers, Sunday School, Youth Association, Vanitha Samajam are active. Church owns a cemetery and plans include a charitable hospital, creche, and vocational training center.
