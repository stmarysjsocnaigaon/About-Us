About Our Church

“With the abundant blessings of God Almighty, our parish continues to grow in faith, fellowship, and service.”

Our History

Beginnings

St. Mary’s Jacobite Syrian Church, Naigaon – Vasai traces back to St. Thomas Syrian Church, Byculla, Mumbai, established in 1975. Families settled in Mira Road, Bhayandar, Naigaon, Vasai, Nallasopara, and Virar. Initially dependent on Byculla and Malad for Holy Qurbana.

Foundation

First Holy Qurbana: 29th Nov 1987 by Rev. Fr. Varghese Valayil. Vasai declared independent: Dec 1998, Rev. Fr. Kuriakose Kadavumbhagam appointed. Land (65 cents) donated by Mr. Antony John Pereira, thanks to Chev. K. V. John.

Growth of the Parish

Foundation stone consecrated: 13th Sep 1999 by H. G. Thomas Mor Themotheos. Altar stone blessed: 10th Apr 2000 by H. H. Moran Mor Ignatius Zakka I. Holy Mooron Consecration: 8–9 Jun 2002. Library inaugurated: Sep 2002 with 5000+ books (thanks to Chev. C. M. Joy).

Holy Soonoro

Our church is privileged to house the Holy Soonoro of Mother Mary. This sacred relic blesses the congregation and inspires devotion. Prayer and intercessory meetings to Mother Mary are held every Wednesday, fostering spiritual growth and fellowship among members.

Present Day

St. Mary’s Church has over 100 families. Services are held every Sunday at Naigaon and Nallasopara. Sunday School, Prayer meetings, Parish Youth Association, and Martha Mariam Vanitha Samajam are actively functioning. The church owns its own cemetery, unique among Jacobite Churches in Mumbai. Future charitable projects include a hospital, creche, and vocational center for women.
